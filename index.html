Have questions? We have answers!

Why should I choose Wozber to build my resume?

Wozber focuses on the things that drive a recruiter's decision: content and quality. We offer you a simple process for writing a high-quality resume with a cover letter, while targeting them to your chosen job positions and styling your resume without sacrificing its readability. You will enjoy the simplicity and our virtual resume expert, the Wozber Guide, will provide you all the needed help along the way.

In which languages can I create my resume?

You can create your resume in one of the following languages: Bulgarian, Danish, Dutch, English (GB), English (US), Finnish, French, German, Indonesian, Italian, Lithuanian, Malay, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Swedish, Turkish, Ukrainian. The support for more languages is coming soon.

How many different resumes and cover letters can I create?

You can create an unlimited number of resumes and cover letters – entirely for free.

How many times can I download my resume?

You can download your resume and cover letter an unlimited number of times – totally for free.

Can I add a custom info block on my resume?

At the moment, it's not possible. We recommend that you stick with the standard blocks of information so that the person reading your resume would know where to look to find the needed data. Conventional content is also necessary to help applicant tracking systems parse your content correctly.

Which information can I include on my resume?

Crafting a compelling resume is crucial, and we've compiled the best industry practices of what to include and what to leave out so that your resume has the highest chances of success. Your resume will feature personal information, work experience, educational background, certificates, skills, languages, and summary. These components are essential and can be added entirely free of charge.

As a Wozber member with an active Custom Templates service, you also have the opportunity to enrich your resume with additional sections: projects, internships, volunteering experiences, courses, conferences, publications, references, awards, licenses, and hobbies (remember to keep them relevant and appropriate for the job application).

Can I edit the content headers?

At the moment, you can only change the language of the content headings. We use well-known titles to optimize your resume so that it would be easy to navigate through it and be easy to parse by candidate filtering software.

Can I download my resume as a .doc file?

At the moment, you can download your resume only in PDF format. It makes the output the highest quality possible.
